How Learning Mode Works
1
Answer & get instant feedback

After you submit, you'll see if you were right or wrong, plus a full explanation with exam traps and key concepts.

2
Rate your confidence

Three buttons appear after each answer:

  • Guessed — you weren't sure. The question comes back sooner for review.
  • Knew it — you were confident. Normal review schedule.
  • Too easy — no challenge at all. The question is pushed further out.
3
Spaced repetition does the rest

Every question lives in a box (1–5). Correct answers promote it; wrong answers reset it. Your confidence rating fine-tunes the timing:

Box Status Next review
1New / WrongSame day
2Shaky1 day
3Learning3 days
4Known7 days
5Mastered21 days
Flag for review

Click the Flag button at the bottom to bookmark any question. Flagged questions appear in your Review Pool for targeted practice later.
